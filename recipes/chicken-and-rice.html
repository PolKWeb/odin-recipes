Chicken and Rice

Description

You can never go wrong with chicken and rice. A staple dish in most cultures, with a surprsingly large amount of possibilies to make it uniquely your own.

This is the perfect dish for anyone trying to keep good shape. A perfect amount of carbs, with a perfect amount of protein.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thigh
  • marinade
  • Rice
  • Veggies of your choice. You can't go wrong
  • Air fryer, if you want an easy life

Steps

  1. Set air fryer to 380 degrees for 15 minutes
  2. Flip chicken to cook well
  3. Make rice
  4. eat
