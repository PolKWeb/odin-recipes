Cereal

Description

A hearty bowl of cereal will bring a smile onto anyones face. Such simplicity, with such flavor. You might think cereal is only meant for the morning... but here, we believe any time, is cereal time.

Ingredients

  • A bowl
  • Milk of your choosing
  • Cereal
  • Fruit, if you're starting the day living on the edge

Steps

  1. It is very crucial to first, add the cereal
  2. Once the bowl is full of the desired amount of cereal, add the milk
  3. Cut a banana and add for some extra flavor and nutrition
